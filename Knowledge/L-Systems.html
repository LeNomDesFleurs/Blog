L-Systems
L-Systems are an algorithmic way of describing the growth of plants and cellular evolution by using recursive instructions that generates fractal visual.

It uses Turtle Geometry : It's a way of drawing by using sequential command. The two main command are go forward and turn . Within a tridimensional context, turn instructions are in the 3 different axis.

L-Systems are composed of 3 things :
  • A Dictionary of all the command e.g. F to go forward, & to rotate clockwise on the X axis, etc...
  • An Axiom (or premise in Touch Designer), it's the pre-existing shape. You'll often want to have something before the algorithm begin it's work, a trunk for your tree for exemple.
  • Rules, sequence of commands that you can call recursively
As an exemple, the default L-system in Touch designer uses this data:
premise:FFFA

A=!"[B]////[B]////B
B=&FFFA
It makes a nice 3D tree
  • !" Are related to size change depending on generation.
  • /& Are rotation commands
  • [] Are pop/push instructions, it lets you draw a branch and get back to drawing the trunk
