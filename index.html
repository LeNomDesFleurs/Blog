~ One Thing

Often times, people recommendation tend to be overwhelming, and it's normal, there is so much great things in the world. But I'd like people to try and make a kind of "essence recommandation" maybe 5 things max, preferably in 5 differente "styles". Can be decided by format : one sound, one image, one 3d file, etc... Or if you are really into music and just music : 5 different genres.

It doens't need to be "your favorite" or "top 1", it can change everyday, it doesn't even need to be something you like, it can be "one news that bothered me" or "one artist that I discovered and whose work I really hate for some reason", something you think gives an hint on who you are and what makes you. A curated list of what is currently resonating in your brain, if you will. I could be part or parallel to the quite widespread now page (https://nownownow.com)

Anyway, all of this is just a vector, draw your segment however you like, the core of the idea is that selecting what you want to show others is for me as important as what you show, and I think there is a broader discussion on wether artist should curate their art, and wether it is part of the artistic process.
